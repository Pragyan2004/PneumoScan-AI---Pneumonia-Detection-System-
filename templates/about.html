About PneumoScan AI

Revolutionizing pneumonia diagnosis through artificial intelligence

Our Mission

PneumoScan AI is dedicated to improving early detection of pneumonia through advanced deep learning technology. Our platform assists healthcare professionals in making accurate and timely diagnoses, ultimately saving lives and improving patient outcomes.

Technology

Powered by state-of-the-art convolutional neural networks (CNNs), our AI model has been trained on thousands of chest X-ray images to recognize patterns associated with pneumonia with high accuracy and reliability.

Model Architecture: Deep CNN with 4 Convolutional Layers
Training Data: 12,000+ Chest X-ray Images
Accuracy: 94.2%
Processing Time: < 3 seconds

Clinical Validation

Our system has undergone rigorous testing and validation by medical professionals, achieving performance metrics that meet clinical standards for pneumonia diagnosis.
